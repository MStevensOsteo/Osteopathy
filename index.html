Meet Michelle

A practitioner who listens

✦ ✦ ✦

Michelle Stevens is a registered osteopathic practitioner based in Kelowna, B.C., with a deep commitment to understanding how the nervous system shapes our lived experience. With a Bachelor of Science, a Diploma in Osteopathy, and ongoing graduate studies in Education, Michelle brings a uniquely integrated perspective to every session — one that honours the whole person, not just presenting symptoms.

Her practice is rooted in compassion, curiosity, and evidence-informed care. Whether you are navigating the aftermath of a brain injury, managing the complexities of neurodivergence, or simply seeking a deeper sense of ease in your body, Michelle provides a calm, unhurried space where healing can genuinely begin. Her approach invites the nervous system to settle — and in that settling, transformation becomes possible.

Philosophy

Neurological attunement,
first.

Before the body can heal, the nervous system must feel safe. Michelle's practice begins with neurological attunement — a careful, respectful process of meeting each client's system exactly where it is. By tuning into the subtle rhythms of the craniosacral system and the patterns held within connective tissue, she creates the conditions for genuine regulation to emerge. This isn't about doing more. It's about listening more deeply, so that the body's own intelligence can lead the way toward recovery and resilience.

Specialization

Michelle's areas of focus

01

Brain Trauma & Concussion

Osteopathic care for post-concussion syndrome, traumatic brain injury, and the lasting effects of neurological disruption on the whole body.

02

Neurodivergent Support

Sensitive, individualized care for clients with ADHD, autism spectrum conditions, dyslexia, and related neurological differences — honouring each person's unique system.

03

Nervous System Regulation

Working with dysregulation, anxiety, and chronic stress patterns through craniosacral therapy and osteopathic mobilization to restore a felt sense of safety.

04

Chronic Pain & Recovery

Addressing persistent musculoskeletal pain, post-surgical recovery, and complex injury patterns with gentle, whole-body osteopathic assessment and treatment.

What I Offer

Services

Osteopathic Manual Therapy

Full-body osteopathic assessment and hands-on treatment addressing structural, visceral, and craniosacral imbalances. Sessions are individualized and deeply attentive.

Craniosacral Therapy

A gentle, non-invasive approach that works with the rhythmic movement of cerebrospinal fluid to release restrictions in the craniosacral system — particularly supportive for neurological conditions.

Holistic Wellness Consultation

An integrative consultation exploring the interplay between body, nervous system, environment, and lived experience — supporting clients in building sustainable pathways to wellbeing.
